## Notes

### Acess

- insertion depends (takes longest to insert at beginning)
- removal depends (takes longest to remove at beginning)
- searching O(n)
- access O(1)

### Methods

- push() O(1)
- pop() O(1)
- shift() O(n)
- unshift() O(n)
- concat() O(n)
- slice() O(n)
- splice() O(n)
- sort() O(n\*logn)
- forEach/map/filter/reduce/etc - O(n)
